Academics


Education is the foundation of the future life of our children. In this, we aim at building our students effectively into the proper academic track that they would be the drive in making the difference in the society. Our school not only promotes thinking in class, but also in real life by taking part in different competitions, seminars and study tours so as to build students in applying the knowledge acquired in class. With sufficient professional staff, we ensure that our students excel academically in the National Exams as well as internal exams by closely follows ups in academic trends, giving advice to our students in improving academic performance and also constant consultations.

Boarding life


We believe that good environment is among the chief factors that boost student’s performance. In this, our school has a beautiful environment made up of enough classrooms, library dining halls and accomodations enough for 1000+ students. We ensure a cleans and sanitary environment by holding our slogan “St.Peter Claver is a green campus, We plant trees, We reuse, We recycle”. Also, the school has proper hostility for students in which students are well-shaped in good behaviours following the Jesuit way of life and also through departments like the boarding that ensures best living conditions, guidance and counselling and the discipline office that maintains discipline among the students.

Co-Curricular Activities



St.Peter Claver is a land of talents. Our students won’t not only excel academically, but also get an opportunity to develop and strengthen their natural talents. Co-curricular activities allow students to build up their passions in which students get involved in different activities such as sports, arts, culture and leadership through clubs, seminars, talent shows, external and internal competitions as well as cooperation with other institutes. We ensure that our students are built in the best foundations to make a difference in the society by involvement in international seminars such as African Leadership Academy Model African Union (ALAMAU) in which new African Leaders are born.

Alumni


St.Peter Claver Alumni is the community of all graduates of St.Peter Claver High School. We aim at returning to the community the values we learnt while at St.Peter Claver High School. We also aim to build love , unity and solidarity among all Claverians for we are all one family. We take part in many activities outside the school like charities, sports and games and also supporting our young brothers and sisters in their studies through guidance and other activies like sports,games,exhibitions and talent shows. We are driven by the Jesuit value ”Magis”, meaning “More”. We give more to the society, that which we acquired from St.Peter Claver High School. Your donations could help us in smoothing our service to the world!
